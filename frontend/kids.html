For grown-ups

No accounts. No names. No collected progress.

Young Word Explorers is a small, kid-friendly doorway into verified word histories. It does not ask children to sign in, type personal information, or save progress on a server.

  • The quiz loop is prediction, surprise, story, connection, and sentence practice across 24 word histories.
  • Four replayable games extend the learning: Then or Now? (sorting meanings in context), Match the Meaning (recall and pairing), Build a Word (morphology and roots), and Pattern Finder (recognizing how meanings change).
  • The family-friendly vocabulary set is grouped by theme and hand-picked for safe home and classroom use.
  • Each quiz reveal keeps the source label visible for teachers and parents.
  • The full adult lexicon search stays on the main PensiveApe explorer.
